Privacy
How will Connectee determine if the content should be removed due to privacy voilations?
Anyone who is faking thier profile, causing harm, being mean or disrespectful on this platform will be removed. Everything everyone does is saved on our database which means that we keep track of what everone is doing.

How does Connectee's privacy process work?
If someone report or messages us based on a complaint on a user, we will be looking into the situation. Based off what we see in the users history we will take the appropriate actions to ensure that everything that they posted does not harm or harass anyone. If there is any issues we will notify the user and give them a warning.

Tips on filling a privacy complaint:
If you have any complaints go to our contacts tab. This tab will allow you to enter in your complaint. When you are writing your complaint make sure you are clear about:
-the individual's name who is violating the terms and conditions
-the date of violation
-any screen shots or proof of what was going on
