History
Monika Szucs started creating Connectee since September 2016.

Before we get into that, let get into a bit of a background about her. She has always been passionate about doing YouTube videos. Throughout her time on Youtube she has met a lot of people around the world. The only problem was it was hard to find people locally to collaborate with. She tried joining Facebook groups and other chat forms online but it seemed like no one was truely passionate about what they do on Youtube. She hoped that someday She could join some kind of a group that could ocnnect people locally easliy and on the go.

Then in September of 2016 she joined the Digital Design and Development Diploma program at BCIT. This program consisted of web development, business, ethics/law, and design coureses. Throughout her program one of the assignment was to create a business. That is when she decided to create Connectee.

She first created the website for Connectee. this website contained all the information to inform current and potential users. Alongside, when you are logged into Connectee you can acess the dashboard. Which is where you can create and help out with projects.

She eventually plans to create an app which will be dedicated to helping users find projects to help out with on the go. These apps might be a while until it comes out because she has many projects going on. However, she is constantly trying her best and triyng to improve herself and Connectee to help everyone out there.

She hopes that this company will be successful and help everyone out there. She is open to any suggestions which you can ask by going to the contacts page to let the company know what improvements should be made.
